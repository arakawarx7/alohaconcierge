About Us
The Surf Bus is a family run tour company based on the North Shore of Oahu, which offers guests a unique type of tour where you actually get off the bus to do something and have some fun! Being North Shore local residents, we'll set you up at the best spots for your activity, so you know you are doing what locals do! As we've watched too many tour buses driving by or stopping for quick snap shots, we realized how much visitors miss out on the North Shore of Oahu. And so, it became our goal to design a Hawaii Tour where you can have a real North Shore experience! All the activities are stuff we do ourselves as part of our daily North Shore living routine, lucky huh? But wait, we want to share it all with you! Our wonderful guides are part of our ohana: Sam, Harry, Keoki and Acey have embraced our philosophy to the fullest and are ready to share with you their Hawaiian knowledge and relaxed island pace to make sure you have the best day on The Surf Bus!
