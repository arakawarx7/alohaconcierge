North Shore Shark Adventures

North Shore Shark Adventures has been safely operating since 2001. We are the original and most established shark adventure tour in Hawaii. The most frequent response we hear from people as they exit the cage is, “This is the best thing I have done on my stay in Hawaii!”. This is sure to be the highlight of your Hawaiian vacation.

We operate shark cage diving daily except Christmas Day and require you to check in 30 minutes prior to your tour start time. Total time from check-in to completion is approximately 2 hours. We drop the cage into the water up to 3 times per tour for about 20 minutes each drop. Each shark cage passenger gets to experience 1 drop.This thrilling experience, the first and finest in Hawaii, will change your life and profoundly affect your feelings toward sharks in Hawaii. 100% SHARK SIGHTINGS ARE GUARANTEED OR YOU DON’T PAY!
